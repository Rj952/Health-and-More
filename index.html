Programmes in Jamaica

NHF

National Health Fund Drug Subsidy

Free/subsidised medications for chronic conditions

Eligible

The NHF Individual Benefits Programme provides subsidised prescription drugs for Jamaicans with hypertension, diabetes, asthma, and arthritis.

How to Apply

1Visit any NHF office or registered pharmacy
2Bring valid ID (passport or national ID)
3Bring recent prescription from your doctor
4Complete the NHF Individual Benefits Form
JADEP

Jamaica Drug for the Elderly Programme

Free medications for Jamaicans aged 60 and over

Age 60+

JADEP provides free medications for Jamaicans 60+ managing chronic conditions at public health facilities island-wide.

NIS

National Insurance Scheme

Pension and retirement benefits for contributors

Eligible

NIS provides weekly pension benefits for eligible Jamaican contributors who have reached retirement age. Claim online or at any NIS office nationwide.

PATH

Programme of Advancement Through Health

Cash transfers for vulnerable households

Income-based

PATH provides conditional cash transfers to poor and vulnerable households, including elderly persons living alone without income support.
